ABOUT GARUT

Welcome to Garut

Nestled in the heart of West Java, Garut is a breathtaking destination where nature's magnificence meets rich cultural heritage. From volcanic peaks and hot springs to pristine lakes and ancient temples, Garut offers an unparalleled journey for the adventurous soul.

Experience authentic Indonesian hospitality, savor world-renowned culinary delights like dodol Garut and succulent lamb, and immerse yourself in traditions that have been preserved for centuries.
